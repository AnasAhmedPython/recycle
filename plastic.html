Plastic Recycling

Why not upcycle your old plastic bottles into small planters. Depending on the size of the bottle you are using you can plant a variety of different small plants such as basil, flowers, or cacti! Follow these simple steps to get started:
1.Cut out the bottom third of a 2-liter bottle.
2.Paint the bottle white or the color of your choice.
3.Fill the bottle with seeds and soil.
