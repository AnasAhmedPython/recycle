Paper Recycling:)

There is a very easy way to upcycle paper like mache bowl.
To make a paper mache bowl, use a plain bowl (glass or
steel) and stick bits of paper all around it until you get the
desired thickness is achieved. Let it dry for at least 10 hours.
And you can add a color (green , yellow and etc..) , a shape
(star, heart and etc..) or stickers to make it look beautiful.
